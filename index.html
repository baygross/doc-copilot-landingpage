Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?
When you share a document with our copilot account, we'll go login and and open up that document the same way any other collaborator might. Then we'll take the content and pass it through OpenAI's GPT Large Language Model to generate feedback based on any guidance or direction you have offered.

Suggestions come back and are inserted via the native google docs comment interface. You can reply to comments to interact directly with the copilot and offer steering and direction.
How do I share documents with Document CoPilot?
Simply share your document with document.copilot@gmail.com directly from your Google Docs sharing settings.

Make sure to check the "notify" checkbox, so the copilot gets alerted and can go drop in.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security, never logging or storing your content beyond the realtime processing as we pass it back and forth to Open AI's GPT model. Further, to permanently revoke our copilot's access, all you need to do is unshare the file!
Can I use Document CoPilot for any topic?
Document CoPilot is versatile and can be used across a wide range of topics. Go crazy!

At this time we only support Google documents, not spreadsheets or slides.
Tips and Tricks
Be sure to give the copilot a clear indication through comments of what you are trying to acheive.

Do you want tactical feedback on grammar and tone? Big picture feedback on structure and argument? etc.

The copilot is preconfigured not to overwhelm you with suggestions, but if you want more feedback just instruct it so. "Give me 10 more comments on sections I can improve." etc
What does it cost?
Document CoPilot is free to demo and experiment with, add it to any doc now! If you find it useful, then after an initial set of free comments we charge a small fee (via secure Stripe link) for ongoing comment generation.
